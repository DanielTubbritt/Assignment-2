Baby Driver (2017) is an action movie that has been written and directed by Edgar Wright. Ansel Elgort plays the role of a hero in the film who has a girlfriend called Debora (Lily James) who is seeking to live a life free from crime. Eiza Gonzalez, Jon Hamm, Jamie Foxx, Jon Bernthal, and Kevin Spacey appear in supporting roles.
